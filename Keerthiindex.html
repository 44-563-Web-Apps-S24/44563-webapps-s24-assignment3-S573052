On The Border

I absolutely adore On The Border restaurant for their mouth-watering Mexican cuisine and charming atmosphere. The Stuffed Jalapenos are my absolute favorite, and I can't get enough of them! The warm and inviting ambiance, coupled with the attentive staff, make it my go-to spot for a perfect snack time.
